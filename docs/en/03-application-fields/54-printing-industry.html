Printing Industry

Definition

Traditional market for masking, positioning, and protection.

Historical Background

1950s: masking tape solved printing masking problems.

Example

Roller cleaning, plate fixing, color masking. Like the beautician of printing.

Formula

Printing Efficiency = f(Masking Effect, Residue Rate, Ease of Use)

Core Concepts

Requirements: precise masking, no residue, easy operation.

Extended Applications

Masking: paper tape; positioning: double-sided; cleaning: adhesive dust roller; protection: PE film; hot stamping: thermal transfer.
