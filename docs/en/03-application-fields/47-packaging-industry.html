Packaging Industry

Definition

Tapes largest application market, for carton sealing, strapping, and protection.

Historical Background

1950s: BOPP packaging tape rapidly dominated with low cost and high efficiency.

Example

Every express parcel uses tape; billions daily consume massive quantities. Guardian of express delivery.

Formula

Packaging Efficiency = Parcel Quantity / Tape Usage

Core Concepts

Demand characteristics: high volume, low price, high efficiency for automated packaging.

Extended Applications

Carton sealing: BOPP (general); heavy-duty: kraft paper; moisture-proof: PE; anti-theft: VOID; eco: kraft paper (biodegradable).
