Furniture Industry

Definition

Important market for edge banding, surface protection, and assembly.

Historical Background

1970s: hot-melt edge banding and protective films improved efficiency.

Example

Panel edge banding, surface protection, drawer rails. Like the beautician of furniture.

Formula

Furniture Tape Value = Aesthetics x Durability x Eco-friendliness

Core Concepts

Requirements: beautiful, durable, eco-friendly. Must withstand daily wear.

Extended Applications

Edge banding: hot-melt (aesthetics); surface protection: PE (anti-scratch); assembly: structural (load-bearing); decorative: decorative; cushioning: foam.
