Industry Standards

Definition

Unified specifications for product technical requirements and test methods.

Historical Background

1960s: national and industry standards regulated market order.

Example

Thickness, width, adhesion all have standard specifications. Like game rules.

Formula

Standard Compliance = f(Technical Indicators, Test Methods, Inspection Rules)

Core Concepts

Market order guarantee: unified standards for fair competition.

Extended Applications

National: GB/T (China); industry: HG/T (chemical); international: ISO/ASTM; enterprise: Q/; group: T/.
