Environmental Regulations

Definition

Laws governing environmental protection in tape production and use.

Historical Background

1990s: stricter regulations drove water-based and solvent-free adhesive development.

Example

EU REACH restricts hazardous chemicals. Like a green mandate driving industry transformation.

Formula

Environmental Compliance = f(Raw Material Selection, Process, Waste Treatment)

Core Concepts

Industry transformation driver: regulations push toward eco-friendly materials and processes.

Extended Applications

EU REACH; US TSCA; China RoHS; Japan CSCL; global trends: VOC limits, biodegradability requirements.
