Storage Conditions

Definition

Environmental requirements for maintaining stable tape performance.

Historical Background

1980s: storage standards defined temperature, humidity, and lighting requirements.

Example

Store in cool, dry place. No sun, no moisture. Like retirement living conditions.

Formula

Storage Life = f(Temperature, Humidity, Lighting, Package Integrity)

Core Concepts

Quality assurance foundation: controlled conditions slow aging.

Extended Applications

Temperature: 5-25C; humidity: 40-60%RH; avoid direct sunlight; keep sealed; ventilated but dust-free.
