Shelf Life

Definition

Duration tape maintains performance under specified storage conditions.

Historical Background

1990s: stabilization technology extended shelf life to 3-5 years.

Example

Tape hardens and yellows over time. Like a freshness date.

Formula

Remaining Performance (%) = Initial Performance x e^(-k x t)

Core Concepts

Aging rate measurement: controlled storage conditions extend shelf life.

Extended Applications

Ordinary: 1-2 years; packaging: 2-3; industrial: 3-5; specialty: 5-10; military: 10+ years.
