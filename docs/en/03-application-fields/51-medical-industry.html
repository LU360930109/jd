Medical Industry

Definition

High-end market for wound care, device fixation, and surgical assistance.

Historical Background

1960s: medical PSAs achieved safe, comfortable, low-allergy bonding.

Example

Bandages, surgical films, electrode pads. Like guardian angels of healthcare.

Formula

Medical Tape Value = Safety x Comfort x Functionality

Core Concepts

Requirements: non-toxic, non-irritating, with specific functions (breathable, waterproof, antibacterial).

Extended Applications

Wound care: bandages (breathable); surgery: surgical film (sterile); electrodes: ECG (conductive); ostomy: flanges (leak-proof); orthopedic: moldable.
