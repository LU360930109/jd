Medical Industry

Details

The medical industry has strict regulatory requirements for adhesive tape. Medical tapes must pass biocompatibility testing (ISO 10993). Bandages use low-sensitivity PSA for sensitive skin; surgical tape requires high strength and breathability; sports tape needs good elasticity and support.
