Automotive Industry

Details

The automotive industry is an important application field for adhesive tape. Interior trim uses acrylic foam tape requiring heat resistance above 80°C; wire harness bundling uses PVC insulation tape requiring vibration and oil resistance; body sealing uses butyl rubber tape requiring weather resistance.
