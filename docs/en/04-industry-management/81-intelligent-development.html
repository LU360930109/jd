Intelligent Development

Definition

Application of intelligent technologies to improve efficiency and management.

Historical Background

2010s: Industry 4.0 concepts drove smart development.

Example

Smart production lines, smart warehouses, smart customer service. Like a digital brain.

Formula

Smart Level = f(Automation Rate, Digitalization Rate, Intelligence Rate)

Core Concepts

Efficiency improvement engine: smart technologies for production and management.

Extended Applications

Smart production: automation, robots, AGV; smart management: ERP, MES, WMS; smart service: online support, remote diagnostics; smart products: sensors, IoT.
