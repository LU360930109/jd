Standardization Regulations

Definition

Institutional system for establishing and implementing standardized management.

Historical Background

1990s: standardized management systems improved efficiency.

Example

From job duties to workflows, every link has standards. Like a management constitution.

Formula

Standardization Degree = Standardized Links / Total Links x 100%

Core Concepts

Management foundation: standards for efficient and consistent operations.

Extended Applications

Technical standards; management standards; work standards; implementation: training, monitoring; periodic review and revision.
