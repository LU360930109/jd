Exhibition Exchange

Definition

Industry exhibitions for display and networking.

Historical Background

1980s: industry exhibitions became important platforms.

Example

China Adhesives Expo, German Adhesives Show. Like industry gatherings.

Formula

Trade Show Value = f(Visitor Count, Transaction Volume, Brand Exposure)

Core Concepts

Industry exchange platform: showcasing capabilities, understanding trends, building connections.

Extended Applications

International: Europe, North America, Asia; domestic: Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen; specialized: adhesives, labels, packaging; technical forums; customer visits.
