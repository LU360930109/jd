Industry Certification

Definition

Third-party proof of product and enterprise industry recognition.

Historical Background

1990s: certification systems improved market trust.

Example

ISO 9001 quality, ISO 14001 environmental certifications. Like quality medals.

Formula

Certification Value = f(Authority, Coverage, Recognition)

Core Concepts

Foundation of market trust: third-party proof of capability and quality.

Extended Applications

Quality: ISO 9001, IATF 16949; environmental: ISO 14001; safety: ISO 45001; product: UL, CE, TUV; industry-specific: medical, food, automotive.
