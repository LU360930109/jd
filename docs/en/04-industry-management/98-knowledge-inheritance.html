Knowledge Inheritance

Definition

Passing professional knowledge and experience to the next generation.

Historical Background

2000s: knowledge management systems improved transfer efficiency.

Example

Technical documentation, training courses, experience sharing. Like a knowledge relay.

Formula

Knowledge Transfer Efficiency = New Employee Readiness Time / Training Time x 100%

Core Concepts

Foundation of industry development: systematic transfer accumulates wisdom.

Extended Applications

Explicit knowledge: documentation, standards; tacit knowledge: skills, know-how; management: knowledge bases, maps; sharing: training, mentoring; innovation: R&D, breakthroughs.
