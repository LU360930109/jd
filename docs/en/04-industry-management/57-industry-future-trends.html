Industry Future Trends

Definition

Predicted directions and driving forces for industry development.

Historical Background

Every transition (natural to synthetic, solvent to solvent-free) drove growth. Future: smart and green.

Example

Future tapes may be smart like smartphones, sensing environment and self-adjusting. Like evolution.

Formula

Industry Growth Rate = f(Technical Innovation, Market Demand, Policy Direction)

Core Concepts

Compass for change: high-performance, green, and smart directions.

Extended Applications

High-performance: stronger, more extreme; green: biodegradable, recyclable, low-carbon; smart: sensing, self-healing; lightweight: thinner, stronger; functional: conductive, thermal, antibacterial.
