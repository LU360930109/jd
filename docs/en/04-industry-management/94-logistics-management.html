Logistics Management

Definition

Warehousing and transportation management from production to delivery.

Historical Background

2000s: modern logistics improved efficiency.

Example

Warehousing, transportation, inventory control. Like a product mover.

Formula

Logistics Efficiency = Order Delivery Cycle / Order Processing Cycle x 100%

Core Concepts

Delivery efficiency guarantee: optimizing logistics for faster, cheaper delivery.

Extended Applications

Warehousing: inbound, outbound, inventory; transport: route planning, mode selection; distribution: last mile; inventory: safety stock, turnover; IT: WMS, TMS systems.
