Industry Talent Demand

Definition

Quantity and quality of talent required for industry development.

Historical Background

2000s: accelerated development led to diversified talent needs.

Example

R&D, production management, marketing, quality. Like the lifeblood of the industry.

Formula

Talent Supply-Demand Ratio = Talent Supply / Talent Demand

Core Concepts

Mirror of industry development: talent needs reflect growth direction.

Extended Applications

Technical: R&D, process, quality; management: production, supply chain, HR; marketing: sales, market, customer service; cross-functional: tech+management; international.
