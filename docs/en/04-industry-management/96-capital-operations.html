Capital Operations

Definition

Using capital tools for enterprise development.

Historical Background

2000s: capital market development made capital operations important.

Example

Financing, M&A, IPO. Like an enterprise accelerator.

Formula

Capital Return = Net Profit / Total Capital x 100%

Core Concepts

Enterprise growth booster: rapid expansion and resource integration through capital.

Extended Applications

Financing: equity, debt; M&A: horizontal, vertical; IPO, backdoor listing; investment: industrial, financial; exit: share transfer, buyback.
