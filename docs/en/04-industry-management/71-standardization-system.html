Standardization System

Definition

Systems for establishing and implementing standards in the enterprise.

Historical Background

1980s: enterprise standardization systems were established.

Example

From raw materials to products, every link has standards. Like a management constitution.

Formula

Standard Coverage = Standardized Links / Total Links x 100%

Core Concepts

Management foundation: establishing standards for efficiency and quality.

Extended Applications

Technical: product, process, testing; management: policies, workflows; work: job responsibilities, SOPs; implementation: training, monitoring; review: periodic revision.
