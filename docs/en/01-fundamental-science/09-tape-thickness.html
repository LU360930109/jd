Tape Thickness

Details

Tape thickness includes backing thickness and adhesive thickness. Total thickness affects tape flexibility, conformability, and bonding performance. Generally, thicker adhesive provides stronger adhesion but higher cost; thicker backing makes the tape stiffer but less flexible.
