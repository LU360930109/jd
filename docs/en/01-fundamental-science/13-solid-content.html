Solid Content

Definition

Proportion of solid components in adhesive, affecting coating weight and drying time.

Historical Background

1950s: oven-drying weighing method became the standard test.

Example

50% solids leaves half thickness after drying; 30% leaves one-third. Like real ingredient ratio.

Formula

Solids Content (%) = Dried Mass / Wet Mass x 100%

Core Concepts

Efficiency indicator: high solids means thicker dried layer at same coating weight.

Extended Applications

Solvent-based: 30-50%; water-based: 40-60%; solvent-free: 95-100%; hot-melt/UV: 100%.
