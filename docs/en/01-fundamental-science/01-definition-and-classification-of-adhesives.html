Definition and Classification of Adhesives

Details

Adhesives are substances that bond two materials together through surface wetting, molecular diffusion, or chemical reactions. The earliest known adhesives date back 5,000 years, when ancient Egyptians used animal glues to build pyramids. The modern adhesive industry began in the 1930s with the invention of synthetic resins.
