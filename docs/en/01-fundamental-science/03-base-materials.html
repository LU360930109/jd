Base Materials

Details

Base materials form the backbone of adhesive tape, providing support and protection for the adhesive layer. Common base materials include: BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) — high transparency, low cost, widely used for packaging tape; PET (Polyester film) — heat resistant, dimensionally stable, used in electronics; PVC (Polyvinyl chloride) — excellent insulation, used for electrical tape.
