Viscosity

Definition

Flow resistance of adhesive, affecting coating and bonding.

Historical Background

1940s: rotational viscometers replaced inaccurate drip-time method.

Example

Water = low viscosity; honey = high. Determines coating uniformity.

Formula

Dynamic Viscosity (Pa.s) = Shear Stress (Pa) / Shear Rate (s-1)

Core Concepts

Governs coating, wetting, filling behavior. 10C increase reduces viscosity ~50%.

Extended Applications

Spray: 50-500 mPa.s; roll-coating: 1,000-5,000; doctor-blade: 10,000-50,000; screen printing: thixotropic.
