Weather Resistance

Definition

Ability to resist aging from UV, ozone, and temperature changes.

Historical Background

1980s: formulations extended outdoor lifespan from months to 10+ years.

Example

Automotive tinting yellows in sun. Like sunscreen for adhesive.

Formula

Outdoor Lifespan = f(UV Stabilizers, Antioxidants, Substrate Weather Resistance)

Core Concepts

Antioxidant capacity: stabilizers slow UV/ozone attack on molecular bonds.

Extended Applications

Indoor: 1-3 years; outdoor: 3-5; construction: 10-15; automotive: 15-20; aerospace: 30+ years.
