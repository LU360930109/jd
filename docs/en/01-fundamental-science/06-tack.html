Tack

Details

Tack is the bonding force generated instantly when the tape is lightly pressed against the substrate. The typical test method is the Rolling Ball Tack Test, where a steel ball rolls down an incline and the rolling distance on the adhesive surface is measured. Shorter distance indicates higher tack.
