Tape Length

Definition

Longitudinal dimension, typically in meters or yards.

Historical Background

1950s: large-diameter coating enabled 1,000+ meter rolls.

Example

Office: 10-20 m; industrial: 500 m; cable wrapping: 5,000 m.

Formula

Number of Uses = Total Length / Single-Use Length

Core Concepts

Balance convenience vs roll-changing frequency.

Extended Applications

Household: 10-30 m; office: 30-100 m; industrial: 100-500 m; specialty: 5,000+ m; military: 100+ m.
