Coating Equipment

Details

Coating equipment is the core equipment in adhesive tape production, mainly including coating heads, drying ovens, winding systems, and control systems. The coating head determines the adhesive application method and precision; the drying oven is for drying and curing; winding systems ensure stable material transport.
