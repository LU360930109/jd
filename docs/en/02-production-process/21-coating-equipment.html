Coating Equipment

Definition

Mechanical apparatus for uniform adhesive application, core of production lines.

Historical Background

1960s: precision machines integrated tension control, monitoring, and auto-adjustment.

Example

Like a precision brush applying uniform coat at high speed. Master painter quality.

Formula

Coating Precision = f(Roller Precision, Tension Control, Speed Stability)

Core Concepts

Balance precision and speed: micron-level accuracy at high speed requires advanced design.

Extended Applications

Roll: packaging; slot-die: optical; micro-gravure: electronic; spray: complex shapes; inkjet: patterned labels.
