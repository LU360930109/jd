Packaging Process

Definition

Protecting finished tape products, affecting appearance and storage stability.

Historical Background

1980s: moisture-proof and vacuum technologies developed.

Example

Like clothing for the product: protecting while convenient. Silent guardian.

Formula

Packaging Quality = f(Material Selection, Sealing, Label Completeness)

Core Concepts

Last line of defense: preventing moisture, dust, and compression damage.

Extended Applications

Simple: cardboard; moisture-proof: PE+desiccant; vacuum: aluminum foil; sterile: medical; military: ballistic cases.
